GHARWALI FEELINGS

Amenities

Power Breakfast

How many times do you skip having breakfast (the most important meal of the day!) Innumerable times, right! We know the banes of your life and have you covered with free breakfast every morning to charge your fuel.

24*7 Security

If you can’t get a night of peaceful sleep without worrying about who would break in, can you really call that place your home? With cctv cameras, biometric admissions, security personnels round the clock and enabling them with the latest tech, we never take safety lightly.

Laundry

Early morning meetings, impromptu travels, date nights or jamming with your gang, we want you to deck up the best without hassling over whether you missed to wash your favourite shirt or dress. Self serviced washing machines as well as dedicated laundry services, you can choose your pick.

Wifi

Our thumb rule: only a battle tested wifi deserves your time. With unlimited access to high-speed internet we want your lightbulb moments to be as random as possible and create, develop & have fun while you are at it.

Concierge

Wouldn’t you love to have a trained hospitality professional take care of all your woes? Well, who wouldn’t! Our efficient staff who have had rock solid experience in the industry will only be a call away from answering and attending to all your concerns.

Housekeeping

Keeping your home spick and span is completely our responsibility. Being die-hard fans of Marie Kondo, we like to tidy with regular cleaning services of your rooms as well as the communal spaces.
